# BP Strategic Research 2026

## 1. Core Strategic Priorities for 2026
*   **Operational Safety & Reliability:** A core focus on "getting the basics right" after the strategy reset.
*   **Resilient Hydrocarbons:** High-grading the portfolio to ensure oil and gas assets are high-margin and lower-carbon.
*   **Growing Low-Carbon Energy:** Accelerating transition businesses (Renewables, Hydrogen) while ensuring they meet return hurdles.
*   **Capital Discipline:** Strict adherence to a capital frame to improve free cash flow conversion.

## 2. Major Capex/Investment Focus
*   **High-Margin Oil/Gas:** Deepwater (Gulf of Mexico, Brazil) and tight gas hubs.
*   **Transition Growth Engines:** EV charging, Bioenergy, and Hydrogen.
*   **Efficiency Spend:** Capex specifically allocated to "turnaround efficiencies and reliability."

## 3. Efficiency / Digital / AI Initiatives
*   **Turnaround Efficiency:** Digital tools to optimize plant shutdowns and maintenance cycles.
*   **Cost Reduction:** Simplifying the organization and using AI to streamline corporate and business-support functions.

## 4. Strategic Hooks for WhiteSpace
*   **The "Basics" Hook:** BP's focus on "operational safety and reliability" is a hook for **asset integrity and planning compliance**.
*   **Transition Planning:** The challenge of balancing "Resilient Hydrocarbons" with "Low-Carbon" requires sophisticated **multi-scenario planning**.
*   **Reliability Targets:** Their focus on "turnaround efficiencies" matches WhiteSpace's **project execution and asset optimization** playbooks.
