# Ecopetrol Strategic Research 2026

## 1. Core Strategic Priorities for 2026
*   **Production Continuity:** Reaffirming a target of ~750,000 boe/d for 2026 despite political and price challenges.
*   **OpEx Competitiveness:** Maintaining "quartile two" performance in lifting costs among global peers.
*   **Energy Transition (TESG):** Integrating "Technology, ESG" as the foundation of the 2026 planning cycle.
*   **Mitigating External Shocks:** Using ROCE and EBITDA margin protections to handle oil price volatility.

## 2. Major Capex/Investment Focus
*   **Resilient Upstream:** Focus on high-return assets in Colombia and the US Permian (via Hocol/JV).
*   **Offshore Colombia:** Long-term exploration and appraisal of natural gas potential in the Caribbean.
*   **Diversification:** Increasing investment in electricity transmission (ISA integration).

## 3. Efficiency / Digital / AI Initiatives
*   **Digital "Lifting Cost" Control:** Using digital monitoring and automation to keep OpEx competitive.
*   **Efficiency in Planning:** The 2026 plan is being built specifically for a "challenging price environment," requiring high-efficiency planning tools.

## 4. Strategic Hooks for WhiteSpace
*   **The Margin Protection Hook:** Ecopetrol's focus on "mitigating external effects" is a hook for **margin-at-risk analysis and asset optimization**.
*   **Efficiency Leader Positioning:** Since they value quartile-two performance, WhiteSpace can position itself as the tool to **keep them in the top quartiles** through digital excellence.
*   **Complex Transformation:** Balancing oil, gas, and transmission (ISA) requires a **unified strategic planning** framework, matching WhiteSpace's capabilities.
