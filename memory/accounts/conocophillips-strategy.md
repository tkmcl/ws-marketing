# ConocoPhillips Strategic Research 2026

*   **Permian Dominance:** Accelerating production in the Permian while maintaining high capital efficiency.

*   **Structural Efficiency:** Focus on reducing "adjusted operating costs" through better operational planning and execution.
*   **Drilling Technology:** Using advanced data analytics to support the shift toward longer laterals and higher "days-to-depth" performance.

*   **Planning Complexity in Permian:** The shift to 90% 2-mile laterals increases **drilling & completions (D&C) planning complexity**. WhiteSpace can offer solutions for optimized scheduling and resource allocation.
