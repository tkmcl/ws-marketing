# Shell Strategic Research 2026

## 1. Core Strategic Priorities for 2026
*   **Performance and Simplification:** Continued focus on delivering more value with less emissions, emphasizing "fixing" underperforming segments.
*   **Chemicals & Refinery Repositioning:** A key priority for 2026 is the turnaround and repositioning of the Chemicals business following recent margins challenges.
*   **Portfolio High-Grading:** Completing exits from lower-margin assets (e.g., Nigeria Onshore, Canadian Oil Sands) to focus on Integrated Gas and Deepwater.
*   **Disciplined Capital Allocation:** Targeting $20-22B Capex for 2026, consistent with 2025 levels.

## 2. Major Capex/Investment Focus
*   **Integrated Gas:** Maintaining a leadership position in LNG, supported by the Pavilion acquisition.
*   **Deepwater:** Scaling high-margin upstream assets as the core cash engine.
*   **Shareholder Returns:** Aggressive share buyback program ($3.5B) continuing into Q1 2026.

## 3. Efficiency / Digital / AI Initiatives
*   **Operational Performance:** Addressing "operational performance" gaps in the Chemicals segment via digital optimization.
*   **Digital Transformation:** Focus on simplifying the operating model and reducing corporate overhead through digital workflows.

## 4. Strategic Hooks for WhiteSpace
*   **"Fixing" the Portfolio:** The specific mention of fixing/repositioning the Chemicals business is a hook for **asset turnaround and performance monitoring** solutions.
*   **Simplification Mandate:** Shell's push for simplification creates an opening for WhiteSpace to offer **leaner planning and reporting frameworks**.
*   **Deepwater Complexity:** As Shell focuses more on deepwater, managing the **integrated planning for subsea assets** becomes a strategic priority.
