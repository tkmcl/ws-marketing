*   **Integration of Wintershall Dea:** Finalizing the transformative acquisition to become a major international player.

## 3. Efficiency / Digital / AI Initiatives
*   **Digital Integration:** Unifying systems and data across the newly expanded global footprint.
*   **Operational Synergies:** Identifying cost-saving opportunities from the Wintershall Dea merger.
