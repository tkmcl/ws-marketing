# Petoro Strategic Research 2026

## 1. Core Strategic Priorities for 2026
*   **NCS Value Maximization:** As the manager of the SDFI (State's Direct Financial Interest), Petoro's 2026 focus is ensuring the Norwegian Continental Shelf (NCS) continues to be a stable energy supplier to Europe.
*   **Cost and Investment Discipline:** Pushing partners (like Equinor) for greater efficiency in joint ventures.
*   **CO2 Multi-Project Management:** Actively managing a portfolio of decarbonization and CCS projects on the NCS.
*   **Digital Transparency:** Enhancing data-sharing with partners to improve recovery rates.

## 2. Major Capex/Investment Focus
*   **Field Life Extension:** Investment in brownfield projects to maximize existing infrastructure.
*   **Natural Gas Infrastructure:** Ensuring long-term reliability of gas export systems.
*   **NCS Electrification:** Sponsoring power-from-shore projects to lower the carbon footprint of production.

## 3. Efficiency / Digital / AI Initiatives
*   **Partner Data Integration:** Using "digital twins" and shared data models across the NCS to identify efficiency gaps in JVs.
*   **Resource Management:** AI tools to optimize reservoir management and drilling profiles across non-operated fields.
