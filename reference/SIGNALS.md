## What's NOT a Signal

Don't report noise. Skip:
- Generic "company X is committed to innovation" PR fluff
- Routine operational updates with no strategic implication
- Signals older than 7 days (unless part of a developing pattern)
- Anything you can't tie back to a WhiteSpace commercial opportunity or competitive threat
